Dr. Anthony Craig graduated form Loma Linda University School of Dentistry in 2013. He has been practicing in the Inland Empire ever since.

Besides all of the typical dental servies you might expect (fillings, crowns, dentures, etc.), he has extensive training in CAD/CAM dentistry, as well as treatment planning and restoring dental implants.

Dr. Craig grew up in Central California, and attended Pacific Union College near Napa, CA for his undergraduate traning. There he met his wife, Allison. Currently, Dr. Craig lives with Allison, and their son dog, Smokey, near Loma Linda, CA.

When he's not at the office, Dr. Craig enjoys all things tech. (He even built this website!) He plays bass guitar for a few of the local churches, and knows more about Star Wars than any dentist should.

If coming to the dentist is something you dread, fear not! Dr. Craig is ready to take you on—no matter how difficult you think your situation might be.

Taking care of your dental issues is very important. But what's more important is making patients feel comfortable. Dr. Craig strives to make every patient feel welcome and safe.
