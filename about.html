Information

Dr. Anthony Craig works with his wife, Dr. Allison Craig, in Dixon, CA, at Maple Leaf Dental. The Drs. Craig live in Vacaville with their daughter, Autumn, and their dog, Wilson.

In addition to routine dental services such as fillings, crowns, and dentures, Dr. Craig has a thorough understanding of CAD/CAM dentistry, and can restore most dental implants.

Dr. Craig grew up in Central California. In 2009, he graduated from Pacific Union College, near Napa, California. After college, he matriculated to Loma Linda University School of Dentistry, in Loma Linda, California, and graduated in 2013.

When he's not working, Dr. Craig enjoys mostly old things: air-cooled Porsche's, vintage glass, classic Apple/NeXT hardware, and mechanical watches as well. He plays some guitar, and knows more about Star Wars than any dentist should.

If coming to the dentist is something you dread, fear not—Dr. Craig is ready to help!

Taking care of the patient's dental issues is very important. But what is even more important is making that patient feel welcome, comfortable, and safe.
